Vantara: Star of the Forest

The world's largest animal rescue, rehabilitation, and conservation initiative by Reliance Foundation.

Our Commitment to Wildlife

Global Rescue & Care

Spanning over 3,000 acres, Vantara is a sanctuary in Jamnagar, Gujarat. We provide a safe haven and state-of-the-art medical care to over 1.5 lakh animals and 2,000+ species rescued from peril globally.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

The facility features a world-class wildlife hospital, advanced veterinary units, and specialized care centers, including hydrotherapy for elephants, ensuring every animal receives dignity and expert treatment.

Conservation & Research

We actively engage in ex-situ and in-situ conservation, breeding programs, and habitat restoration. Collaborating with international bodies to safeguard endangered species and strengthen global biodiversity.

The Four Pillars of Vantara

1. Rescue

Locating and securing wildlife in distress from abuse, illegal trafficking, or conflict zones, with precision and compassionate care.

2. Treatment & Care

Holistic and advanced medical attention, combining modern diagnostics with traditional therapies like Ayurveda, for comprehensive healing.

3. Rehabilitation

Helping rescued animals regain essential survival skills and transition back to a life of independence.

4. Reintroduction

Careful rewilding and reintroduction efforts for species back into their natural habitats where possible, restoring ecological balance.
